Our Story

For over 30 years, Jian, a devoted father of two and passionate restaurateur, has poured his heart into making Hong Kong City a beloved dining spot in the community. Driven by a dream to share the authentic flavors of his homeland, he came to America to blend the rich culinary traditions of Chinese cuisine with the vibrancy of American culture.

Jian’s journey is one of resilience and vision, fueled by his belief in the power of food to bring people together. Through decades of hard work and dedication, he’s created more than just a restaurant—it’s a place where families gather, friendships grow, and traditions are celebrated.

Hong Kong City stands as a testament to Jian’s unwavering commitment to honoring his roots while embracing the beauty of new beginnings.
